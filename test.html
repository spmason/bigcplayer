Cosmonauts - Stardust

I’m trying to do some catching up with the blog today and this track is far over-due a post. Released in March, it’s a beautiful piece of cosmic nu-disco. There’s a Royksopp feel to the vocal, along with so many carefully crafted synth sounds going on, simple beats with delicately placed toms and the cosmonaut magic touch.
I’ve featured Cosmonauts’ tracks before, which have all been great - but this feels like their anthem has finally arrived.

Mark Foster, A-Trak, & Kimbra - Warrior

Converse have brought together three musicians of the moment to collaborate on a song. Mark Foster of Foster the People, A-Trak of Fool’s Gold & Duck Sauce, and Kimbra of Somebody That I Used to Know fame have all joined into one WARRIOR!

This song is a fun piece of pop and sounds like what you’d expect from a collab of the three. It’s not pushing any boundaries, but it’s not hurting any ears either. The chorus is pretty anthemic. Maybe better than the song is the super-fun wrestling video. Watch above.

Figure of 8 Feat. Sophie Galpin - No One Cries For Me (Worship remix)

I’ve posted the brilliant Mighty Mouse remix of this already and Figure of 8 are doing great things, so when one of my new favourite producers of the year comes up with a remix, will he bring anything new to the table?

Luckily he will - phew, I’ve bigged this guy up so much I’ve got a lot riding on him :) - He changes the vibe from the MM remix, taking it upbeat and more bouncy dancefloor. It starts out with a Serge Gainsbourg feel to it and then my favourite disco piano comes out and it’s all good.

I’m happy with my 2012 discovery!

The Draughtsman - Geschmacklos (Cosmo Vitelli Remix)

I’m really enjoying this surprise track from Cosmo Vitelli. It starts off a bit like the Peter Gunn theme tune, but over nearly 8 minutes CV add layers of percussion, synths and instrumentation to take us on a journey - based on the initial guitar groove. It all gets a bit psychedelic towards the end, but that’s no bad thing. Crazy remix in a very good way.
